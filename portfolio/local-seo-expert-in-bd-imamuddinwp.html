What is Local SEO?

Local SEO is one kind of Search Engine Optimization - (SEO); A strategy that helps your business be more visible in local search results on search engine. Any business that has a physical location or serves a geographic area can benefit from local SEO.

Why Local SEO: → Local SEO allows you to put important information about your site online, such as the address and phone number of your business. This helps increase your business' exposure on the web, which can make your business better known to residents in the area.

Local SEO Citation: → Local citations are online mentions of a business. Citations are a very important factor for local search rankings.
