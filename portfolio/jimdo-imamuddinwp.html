What is Jimdo?

Jimdo is a popular website-builder and all-in-one hosting solution, designed to enable users to build their own websites without any web design experience. Founded in 2007; Headquartered in Hamburg, Germany, with offices in Munich and Tokyo.

What is the use of Jimdo?: → Jimdo is a popular, simple, clean and easy-to-use website builder for Responsive Websites, online stores, bookings, logos, SEO, analytics, domains and hosting.

What Wakes Jimdo Unique? → Jimdo Creator is a drag and drop style website builder and hosting service which is suitable for users with some coding skills. It uses artificial intelligence to get to know the user, their business, and their goals, then builds a website that is already fully personalized to meet their needs.
